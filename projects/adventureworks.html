Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes AdventureWorks sales data to uncover key insights and trends in orders, revenue, and customer behaviour. The dashboard explores sales performance across categories and territories, revealing the data-driven stories behind each metric.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

  • Track sales performance across product categories
  • Analyze revenue by geographic territory
  • Monitor sales trends over time
  • Compare customer engagement across regions

Key Features & Analysis

  • Sales Metrics

    Tracking orders by date, tax, freight, subtotal, and total sales metrics to understand overall business performance.

  • Product Analysis

    Comparing revenue across categories, with clothing seeing the highest order volume while bikes drive the most revenue.

  • Territory Performance

    Identifying Australia as the leader in order volume, showcasing the region's strong engagement with AdventureWorks.

  • Trend Visualization

    Analyzing sales, customer count, and order count over time to identify revenue growth and market trends.

  • Data Modeling

    Creating efficient structures for analysis including date tables and star schema implementation.

Technical Implementation

The dashboard was built in Power BI with:

  • Data modeling to connect sales, product, and territory tables
  • DAX measures for calculating sales KPIs and performance metrics
  • Interactive filters and slicers for dynamic territory analysis
  • Custom visualizations to highlight category performance
  • Time intelligence functions for trend analysis
