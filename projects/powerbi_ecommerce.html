Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides actionable insights for an eCommerce business by analyzing key performance indicators (KPIs) including customer metrics, profitability, shipping costs, and the relationship between product quantities and business outcomes.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

  • Understand customer purchasing patterns and segmentation
  • Optimize shipping strategies based on product quantities
  • Analyze profit margins at both item and order levels
  • Simulate different shipping cost scenarios to maximize profitability

Key Features & Analysis

  • Customer Analytics

    Tracking unique customer counts, growth over time, and segmentation by geographic region and purchase behavior to identify valuable customer groups.

  • Profitability Metrics

    Calculating total profit and profit margin percentage by subtracting landed cost and shipping from sales price, with visualizations showing trends over time.

  • Shipping Cost Optimization

    Analyzing how shipping costs vary with product count, implementing bulk shipping discounts, and evaluating their impact on overall profitability.

  • What-If Scenarios

    Interactive simulations to test how different shipping cost structures would affect profit margins, particularly for high-volume orders.

  • Seasonal Trend Analysis

    Using time intelligence functions to identify seasonal patterns in sales and customer acquisition for better inventory and marketing planning.

Technical Implementation

The dashboard was built in Power BI with:

  • Data modeling to connect customer, product, and sales tables
  • DAX measures for calculating KPIs like profit margin and shipping cost factors
  • Interactive filters and slicers for dynamic analysis
  • Custom visualizations to highlight key relationships
  • What-if parameters for scenario testing
