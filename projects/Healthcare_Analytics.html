A Power BI analysis of patient encounters, insurance coverage, and medical conditions

Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides insights into patient demographics, insurance coverage, and medical conditions across healthcare organizations. By analyzing encounter data, costs, and payer trends, it helps healthcare providers optimize resource allocation and patient care strategies.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

  • Track patient encounter patterns and durations
  • Analyze insurance coverage distribution
  • Identify common medical conditions by demographic
  • Monitor healthcare costs and payer contributions

Key Features & Analysis

  • Patient Demographics

    Analyzing gender (63% of uninsured patients are female), race, and age distributions (Medicare patients average 80+ years).

  • Payer Insights

    Identifying Medicare as the dominant payer, covering over twice the amount of any other insurance type.

  • Condition Analysis

    Highlighting Hyperlipidemia & Chronic Congestive Heart Failure (avg age: 75+) and Normal Pregnancy (avg age: 34) as most common encounters.

  • Cost Tracking

    Comparing base encounter costs, payer coverage, and total claim costs across different patient groups.

  • Geospatial Trends

    Heat maps showing patient distribution and encounter frequency by location.

Technical Implementation

The dashboard was built with:

  • Excel for data cleaning and patient record standardization
  • Power BI data modeling connecting patient, encounter, and payer tables
  • DAX measures for calculating cost metrics and demographic distributions
  • Custom visualizations for condition prevalence and payer comparisons
  • Interactive filters for dynamic analysis by provider and patient group
